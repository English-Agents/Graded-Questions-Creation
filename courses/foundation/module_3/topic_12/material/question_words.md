# Question Words

##Question Words

###Definition

*Words that are used to introduce questions in a sentence. They are used to seek information, clarification, or details about a particular topic or subject.*





####Question words: Set 1


| Question Words | Indication                                       | Example 1                                 | Answer            | Example 2                                      | Answer              |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Who            | Used to ask about a person or people.            | Who will pick me from the railway station? | My brother          | Who is invited to the party?                   | My close friends      |
| What           | Used to ask about specific information.          | What is your name?                        | Alice               | What kind of music do you listen to?           | Pop music             |
| Where          | Used to ask about a place or location.           | Where is my bag?                          | Under the table     | Where did she go?                              | To college            |
| How            | Used to ask about the method, state, or degree.  | How is your preparation for the interview? | It is going good    | How was your childhood?                        | Very happy and memorable |
| Why            | Used to ask about a reason, purpose, or cause.   | Why are you sad?                          | Because I lost my favorite pen | Why did you change your field to IT?           | Because I am good at coding |
| When           | Used to ask about time or duration.              | When is your birthday?                    | On September 22     | When are your exams starting?                  | From the first week of May |
| Whose          | Used to ask about ownership or possession.       | Whose dog is barking in the backyard?     | Our neighbor's      | Whose pen is this?                             | Nayana's              |
| Which          | Used to ask about a specific choice or selection.| Which is your favorite color?             | Black               | Which team are you rooting for in IPL?         | CSK                   |






####Few more examples
```
When  will your train reach Mumbai? At 7:00 P.M.

Who is next to you in this photo? My cousin

Why does he seem upset today? Because his cat died.
```





####Question words: Set 2

####Family of How


| Question Words       | Indication                   | Example 1                            | Answer            | Example 2                              | Answer                  |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ----------------- | -------------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| How much             | Applies to uncountable nouns | How much time will you take?         | 30 mins           | How much work is pending?              | 50%                     |
| How many             | Applies to countable nouns   | How many cookies has he eaten?       | 4 cookies         | How many days to your birthday?        | 2 more days             |
| How far              | Applies to distance          | How far is your college from home?   | 3 km              | How far is the nearest ATM?            | 100 m                   |
| How often            | Applies to frequency         | How often do you visit your grandparents? | Every weekend | How often does he drink water?        | Every two hours         |
| How long             | Applies to time & length     | How long will you take to reach?     | 30 mins more      | How long is the movie?                 | 1.5 hours long          |
| For how long         | Applies to duration          | For how long will the play run?      | For 2 hours       | For how long the activists will protest? | Till their demands are met |
| How (any adjective)  | Applies to most adjectives   | How deep is the lake?                | Not very deep     | How heavy is your luggage?             | 15 kg                   |


####Few more examples
```
How many pencils do you have? 3 pencils

How long  will she take to get here? 20 mins

How important is this project for your career? Very important
```
