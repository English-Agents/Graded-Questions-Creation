### Application of 'Any' & 'Some'

#### **'Any':**
- Used in questions (to ask about the existence or availability of something) and negative sentences.
- Used when there is uncertainty or when the answer could be either yes or no.

**Examples:**

- Have you got any milk?
- We don’t need any support.
- They didn’t sing any songs that we like.

#### **'Some':**
- Used in positive sentences and requests.
- Used when there is certainty or expectation of a positive response.

**Examples:**

- Yes, I have got some milk.
- I want some chocolate.
- Please bring me some water to drink.
- Could you lend me some change?
